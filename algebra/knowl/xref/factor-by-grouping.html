Strategy 1.6.102. Factor by grouping.

  • When does this strategy apply? This strategy usually (but not always) applies when factoring an expression consisting of four terms.
    🔗
    🔗
  • How to apply the strategy
    1. Factor an expression out of the first two terms and factor an expression out of the last two terms.
      🔗
      🔗
    2. In the resulting expression, if both terms have the same binomial factor, then factor this binomial out of both terms.
      🔗
      🔗
