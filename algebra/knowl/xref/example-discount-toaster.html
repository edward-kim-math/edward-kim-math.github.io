Example 1.8.10.

A toaster has a price of \(\$8.40\) but a \(8\%\) discount is being offered.
  1. How much money do you save off the original price?
    🔗
    🔗
  2. What is the amount you actually pay for the toaster?
    🔗
    🔗
🔗
Answer.
  1. \(0.08 \times 8.40\) dollars, which we can simplify to \(0.672\) dollars.
    🔗
    🔗
  2. \(8.40 - 0.08 \times 8.40\) dollars, which we can also write as \((1 - 0.08)8.40\) dollars, or \(0.92(8.40)\) dollarsor \(8.40 - 0.672\) dollars or \(7.728\) dollars.
    🔗
    🔗
