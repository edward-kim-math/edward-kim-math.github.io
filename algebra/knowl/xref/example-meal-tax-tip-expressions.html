Example 1.8.19.

A restaurant meal has a price of \(M\text{.}\) A \(5\%\) tax must be paid, and \(15\%\) tip will be included on the post-tax amount.
  1. How much money is paid in tax?
    🔗
    🔗
  2. How much total money is paid for the meal and tax?
    🔗
    🔗
  3. How much total money is paid in tip?
    🔗
    🔗
  4. How much total money is paid in meal, tax, and tip?
    🔗
    🔗
🔗
Answer.
  1. The tax to pay is \(0.05 \cdot M\) dollars.
    🔗
    🔗
  2. The post-tax amount is \(M + 0.05 \cdot M\) dollars, which is the sum of the meal and the tax.
    🔗
    🔗
  3. The tip is \(15\%\) of the post-tax amount from the previous part. So, the tip is \(0.15 \cdot (M + 0.05 \cdot M)\) dollars.
    🔗
    🔗
  4. The total paid is \(M + 0.05 \cdot M + 0.15 \cdot (M + 0.05 \cdot M)\) dollars.
    🔗
    🔗
🔗
