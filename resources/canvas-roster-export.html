This is based on https://community.canvaslms.com/message/76979-export-student-email. Navigate to the People page, then open the console (also known as Developer Tools) of your browser. (Often found by hitting F12.) Then paste the script below: {% highlight javascript linenos %} var url = '/api/v1/courses/' + (typeof ENV.COURSE !== 'undefined' ?
